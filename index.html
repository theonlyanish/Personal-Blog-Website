About us

Exodus is a privately-held and fully integrated real estate development, investment, asset and property management firm with affiliated design and construction management companies. Whether by lovingly restoring and modernizing a historical property or developing the most modern new construction, Exodus is a brand dedicated to the belief that real estate development is a creative venture and where long-term value is generated at all levels through quality, from innovative and elegant aesthetic design to thoughtful placement of building systems.

Exodus was founded by Anish Kapse in 2010. It all started with a single objective: creating real estate of the highest possible quality. We are proud to say that, only a few years later, we have achieved our goal. Our projects have already made their mark, showing that we are a breath of fresh air in the world of real estate. We are young, ambitious, enthusiastic and always on the lookout for innovative, design-driven and sustainable real-estate concepts. Every member of our team is keen to make a positive impact through the single-minded pursuit of quality and enhanced value.

Current Projects

Osiris


Osiris sits at the heart of downtown Chicago, which features studio and
contemporary-designed units. In addition to the 323 apartments,
this mixed-use project also features the Holiday Inn hotel brand.
The hotel components features a variety of conveniences, services and
amenities including dining and drinking venues, lounges, outdoor pool deck,
and a state-of-the-art 5,000 square foot fitness center.
Apartment residents can enjoy the amenities offered to hotel guests in addition to
apartment resident-only amenities including kitchen/dining/grilling
features, outdoor and indoor lounges, firepits, indoor workspaces, bicycle
storage and indoor dog run and grooming areas. 31% booked as of Oct 2020.

The Cathedral of Saint Mary


The cathedral is located in the Cathedral Hill neighborhood of Chicago.
The cornerstone was laid on December 13, 2016, and the cathedral was
completed three years later.
On 2nd May, 2018, the cathedral was blessed In Chicago, it has become
a landmark that annually draws thousands of people to this sacred
structure, which combines the rich traditions of the Catholic faith
with modern technology.
